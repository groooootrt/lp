Secure Private Access

Avast Secure Private Access replaces the traditional VPN with a cloud-based zero trust network access solution that lets users seamlessly access business applications from any device, anywhere, at any time. It empowers companies to separate their security from the data center and move it to the scalability and reliability of the cloud.

Benefits:

Provides seamless access between applications and devices.
Simple management, unlimited scalability, and strong protection against DDoS attacks.
Authorized users have access to specific private apps without the need to access the network, reducing the risk of lateral movement and the spread of ransomware.
Simple and scalable deployment, eliminating infrastructure overhead.

Secure Web Gateway

Our fully scalable, cloud-based web security solution blocks web threats before they hit the network. Secure your SMB’s network traffic without the complexity and overhead of proxy servers or on-premise appliances.

Just point your DNS to us to leverage our enterprise-grade network security services.

Benefits:

Provides advanced protection - any threat detected in our cloud is immediately blocked for your customers within our network.
Offers quick and easy installation. Get up and running with just three easy steps, and deploy within minutes from the CloudCare platform.
