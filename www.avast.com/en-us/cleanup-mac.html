What we clean:

Crash reports and junk files
MacOS and 3rd party applications automatically create temporary files, which aren’t critical but waste lot of drive space. They include leftover data from applications you may have uninstalled ages ago or programs like Xcode or Safari.
Browser caches
Safari®, Chrome®, and Firefox® browsers store non-critical temporary files and cookies in a cache folder. Deleting this cache not only frees up drive space, but can help protect your privacy.

Applications create logs to record normal operation or how they crashed. In most cases you won’t need them, so why keep them?

FAQ

Do Macs need disk cleanup software?
Macs and PCs are very different in some regards, but in others, they are surprisingly similar: for example, both Apple and Window devices create “junk files” over time, which crowd the device’s memory and prevent you from saving the apps or files that you care about. In that respect, both Macs and PCs need help removing junkware from their system, which is why, yes, Macs do need disk cleanup software.

If you’re uncertain whether your Mac specifically needs cleanup software, we can tell you how to check your Mac storage.

How do I optimize my Mac’s hard drive?
If you are looking for ways to clean up your Mac, empty out its hard drive, or improve its performance, there are a few things you can do.

  • You can defrag your Mac’s hard drive
  • You could upgrade to an SSD from an HDD
  • Empty your trash
  • Delete apps, photos, and videos you no longer use or enjoy
Or, of course, you can use an optimization tool like Avast Cleanup Premium for Mac, which is the easiest and fastest way to make your Mac’s hard drive as optimized as it can be.

What makes Avast Cleanup Premium one of the best cleaner apps for Mac?
Avast Cleanup Premium for Mac could be called the best Mac cleaning software for a number of reasons. For one, we make it easy to find and get rid of bloatware with a few simple clicks. We’re lightweight and fast-performing, so we’ll do our job quickly and we won’t slow down your Mac even while we’re mid-scan. And of course, this cleanup tool is designed by the experts at Avast, who have over 30 years of experience creating secure, powerful software.

Will Avast Cleanup Premium speed up my Mac?
It is possible that Avast Cleanup Premium for Mac will give your Mac a speed boost. It was designed to improve Mac speed and performance by removing junk and unwanted files. If you’re unsure whether your Mac’s performance can be improved, then we recommend you test if your Mac is running slowly.
